What is Ingress?

Ingress is used to expose the website to the world.

Why do we use Ingress?

Ingress is used as a load balancer. Though we have a load balancer service, we use Ingress because it is cost-effective and intelligently routes traffic to requested services.

What is a Load Balancer Service?

A load balancer service routes traffic in a round-robin basis to specific services. For example, a service dedicated to hosting images will receive image-related requests.

Why use Ingress if we have a Load Balancer?

We use Ingress because standard load balancer services in Kubernetes lack enterprise-level features found in tools like nginx or F5, such as advanced routing and SSL termination.

An Ingress may be configured to provide externally-reachable URLs, load balance traffic, terminate SSL/TLS, and offer name-based virtual hosting.

Ingress uses an Ingress Controller.
